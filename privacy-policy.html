Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 9, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Rick and Morty App ("we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Our app only displays publicly available information about Rick and Morty characters, episodes, and locations obtained from the official Rick and Morty API.

Data Usage

The app uses the following types of data:

  • Character Information: Names, images, status, species, and other character details
  • Episode Information: Episode titles, air dates, and episode numbers
  • Location Information: Location names, types, and dimensions

All this information is publicly available and obtained from the official Rick and Morty API.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Rick and Morty API: We fetch character, episode, and location data from the official Rick and Morty API (https://rickandmortyapi.com/)
  • Railway: Our backend service is hosted on Railway platform

Data Storage

We do not store any personal data on our servers. All data displayed in the app is fetched in real-time from the Rick and Morty API.

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
